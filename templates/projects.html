Rocket Simulator
Using the image manipulation techniques from the midterm, I was able to take images of model rocket motor thrust curves and obtain the thrust data. I then wrote a simulator similar to the projectile simulator from homework 3 to simulate the flight of a model rocket on said motor. The end result was a library that would get thrust data, simulate flight, plot data, and even save and load rockets that were previously used.
